About IDPC

We are excited to announce our upcoming Inter-Departmental Programming Contest, a thrilling competition designed to bring out the best in our talented coders from various departments. This event aims to foster collaboration, showcase programming skills, and encourage innovative problem-solving among our students.

Eligibility:

  • Open to all students currently enrolled in any department of Islamic University.
  • Each team must consist of members from the same department.

    • Why Participate?

  • Sharpen Your Skills: Improve your coding and problem-solving abilities.
  • Collaborate and Network: Work closely with peers and make new connections.
  • Boost Your Resume: Gain valuable experience and add a noteworthy achievement to your resume.
  • Win Exciting Prizes: Compete for amazing prizes and bragging rights.
